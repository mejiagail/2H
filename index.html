Definition

he act, process, or occurrence of streaming data or of accessing data that is being streamed, according to Merriam-Dictionary, Webster's is known as "streaming." The phrase refers to the continuous transfer of audio and video files from a server to a client. In video streams, information is transmitted over the internet in a compressed form and is instantly viewed by the viewer. A constant stream of data is used to send the material, which is played as it comes in. Both live performance and recording are options.
